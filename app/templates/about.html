About LearnByEmail

Learn something new every day, delivered straight to your inbox

Our Mission

At LearnByEmail, we believe that learning should be accessible, bite-sized, and fit seamlessly into your daily routine. Our mission is to empower curious minds with concise, high-quality educational content delivered directly to your inbox on topics you're passionate about.

How It Works

Our platform is simple yet powerful:

  1. Choose Your Topics - Tell us what you want to learn about - from programming to philosophy, history to health.
  2. Set Your Schedule - Pick the time of day you'd like to receive your learning content.
  3. Learn Daily - Receive bite-sized, educational content crafted specifically for your interests at your preferred time.

Our Technology

LearnByEmail leverages advanced AI technology to create personalized, educational content that's both accurate and engaging. Our system:

  • Generates unique content based on your specific topic
  • Adapts over time to focus on areas within your topic that might interest you
  • Delivers content exactly when you want it, respecting your time zone and schedule

Our Team

LearnByEmail was created by a small team of passionate educators and technologists who believe in the power of continuous learning. We're dedicated to making education accessible, engaging, and tailored to your personal learning journey.
